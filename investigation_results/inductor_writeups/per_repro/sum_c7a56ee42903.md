## Gap Diagnosis

The oracle differs from Inductor only by using a hand-written Triton split
reduction over the contiguous channel slabs, accumulating per-tile partials for
both returned channels and then finalizing the two output elements. Inductor
already emits the same high-level lowering shape for this repro:
`triton_red_fused_sum_0` plus `triton_per_fused_sum_1`.

Inductor cannot unlock a distinct missing-pattern win here because the compiled
region is already the full input read plus a tiny output write. There is no
multi-output producer sharing, scatter-reduce, algebraic elimination,
recompute-fusion opportunity, or dependent epilogue omitted by the scheduler.
The relevant Inductor change would be lower-level reduction-template tuning for
this very small-output, large-input shape; the current evidence says the repro
is effectively bandwidth/template-constant-factor bound.
